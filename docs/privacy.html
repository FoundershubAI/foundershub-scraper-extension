Privacy Policy — FoundersHub Data Scraper

Effective date:

1) Who we are

FoundersHub Data Scraper (the “Extension”). Contact: support@your-domain.com. Website: https://your-domain.com.

2) What we collect

  • Page content you explicitly choose to scrape (e.g., visible profile fields).
  • Basic extension usage events (e.g., button clicks, error states).
  • Authentication tokens necessary to access your account.

We do not collect your browsing history.

3) How we use data

  • Provide core functionality (scraping selected fields and saving to your account).
  • Authenticate your account and maintain session state.
  • Improve reliability and troubleshoot issues.

4) Data sources

Only pages you visit and choose to scrape. The Extension does not operate on pages you do not open or authorize via Chrome permissions.

5) Where data is processed

  • In the browser: extraction, normalization, and display.
  • On our servers: secure HTTPS APIs at https://api.foundershub.ai (or your configured API domain) for your account.

6) Storage and retention

  • Authentication tokens are stored locally using Chrome storage and cleared on logout.
  • Scraped data you choose to save remains in your account until you delete it or your account is closed.

7) Sharing and selling

We do not sell your data. We do not share data with third parties, except subprocessors necessary to operate our service (e.g., hosting), bound by contractual safeguards.

8) Security

  • HTTPS for all network communication.
  • Access control via JWT tokens.
  • Least‑privilege Chrome permissions (activeTab, scripting, tabs, storage, and host permissions for supported sites and the API domain).

9) Your controls

  • You decide when to scrape and what to save.
  • Clear local data by logging out (tokens removed from Chrome storage).
  • Request deletion of account data via support@your-domain.com.

10) Children

Not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11) International transfers

Data may be processed in regions where we or our subprocessors operate. We apply contractual safeguards as required.

12) Changes

We may update this policy. We will update the effective date and, where required, provide notice.

13) Contact

Email: support@your-domain.com
